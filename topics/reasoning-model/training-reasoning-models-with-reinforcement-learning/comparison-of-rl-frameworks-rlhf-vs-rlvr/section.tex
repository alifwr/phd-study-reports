\subsection{RLVR Practical Advantages}

\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Deterministic and Reproducible:} Avoids the inconsistency inherent in human annotations.
    \item \textbf{Domain Specificity:} Particularly effective for domains with reliable verification signals, such as mathematics and programming.
    \item \textbf{Resource Efficiency:} Removes the cost of maintaining a separate reward model often comparable in size to the base LLM.
\end{itemize}
